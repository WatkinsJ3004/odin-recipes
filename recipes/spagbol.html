Spagbol

Description

Easy comfort food for the whole family to use up spare ingredients. A hearty favourite!

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 1 Carrot
  • Handful Mushrooms
  • Bolognaise sauce
  • Worcester sauce

Steps

  1. Chop the onion, carrots & mushrooms into small pieces
  2. Fry the onion for 5 mins until soft
  3. Add in the minced beef and cook until browned
  4. Add in the chopped carrot & mushroom. Put the lid on and let steam for 10 mins
  5. Add a splash of Worcester sauce to to mix and fry for 2 mins
  6. Add in jar of Bolognaise sauce, simmer for 30 mins
  7. Serve with Spaghetti & Garlic bread. Bosh!
