Beans on Toast

Description

An all-time British classic made only from staples within the cupboard! This simple and nutritious meal sustains all sorts across the nation!

Ingredients

  • 1 can Baked Beans
  • Butter
  • Mature Cheddar cheese
  • Ground black pepper
  • Smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Add a bit of butter to a medium saucepan and melt
  2. Add can of beans to saucepan & bring to boil
  3. Reduce heat to a simmer & add some smoked paprika & a dash of pepper
  4. Cook the beans on a low heat with the lid on for 10 mins
  5. Toast 2 slices of bread
  6. Butter the toast and add the beans
  7. Grate some cheddar over the top and add a dash of pepper
  8. Bon Appetite!
